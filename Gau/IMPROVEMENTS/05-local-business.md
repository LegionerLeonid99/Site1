# IMPROVEMENT PLAN: Local Business & Location Features

## Current Issue
- No specific business address or location
- No service area information
- No local SEO optimization
- Generic contact information

## Proposed Additions

### 1. Business Location Page
- Dedicated "About Us" page with:
  - Business address and contact info
  - Service area map
  - Hours of operation
  - Team/technician profiles
  - Certifications and licenses

### 2. Service Area Coverage
- Interactive map showing service areas
- Neighborhood/city-specific pages
- "Areas We Serve" section
- Local keyword optimization

### 3. Local SEO Elements
- Google My Business integration
- Local business schema markup
- NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency
- Local review integration
- Service area schema

### 4. Location-Specific Content
- "Appliance Repair in [City]" pages
- Local landmarks and references
- Community involvement content
- Local testimonials and case studies

### 5. Contact Information Enhancement
- Multiple contact methods
- Service request scheduling
- Emergency contact procedures
- Response time commitments

## Implementation Priority: HIGH
- Essential for local business success
- Improves local search rankings
- Builds trust and credibility
