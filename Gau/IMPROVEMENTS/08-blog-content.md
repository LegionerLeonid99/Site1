# IMPROVEMENT PLAN: Blog & Content Marketing

## Business Opportunity
- Appliance repair businesses benefit greatly from educational content
- "How to" guides and maintenance tips drive traffic
- Positions business as expert authority

## Proposed Blog Features

### 1. Content Categories
- Appliance maintenance tips
- Troubleshooting guides
- Seasonal appliance care
- Energy efficiency tips
- New appliance reviews
- Repair vs replacement guidance

### 2. Technical Implementation
- Markdown-based blog system
- Category and tag filtering
- Search functionality
- Related posts suggestions
- Social sharing buttons

### 3. SEO-Focused Content
- Long-tail keyword targeting
- Local SEO content
- FAQ-style articles
- Problem-solution content structure

### 4. Content Examples
- "5 Signs Your Dishwasher Needs Repair"
- "How to Maintain Your Coffee Machine"
- "Winter Appliance Care Tips"
- "When to Repair vs Replace Your Washing Machine"

### 5. Content Management
- Easy content creation workflow
- Editorial calendar
- Author profiles
- Content categories

## Implementation Benefits
- Improved SEO rankings
- Customer education
- Trust building
- Lead generation
- Reduced support inquiries

## Implementation Priority: LOW
- Good for long-term growth
- Requires ongoing content creation
- Lower immediate impact than other improvements
