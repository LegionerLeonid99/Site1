# Open Graph Images for Social Media

This folder should contain images for social media sharing:

## Required Images:

1. **og-image.jpg** (1200x630px)
   - Main social media sharing image
   - Should include your business name and key services
   - Used for Facebook, LinkedIn, and general social sharing

2. **twitter-card.jpg** (1200x600px)  
   - Twitter-specific sharing image
   - Can be the same as og-image.jpg
   - Used when people share your website on Twitter

3. **favicon.ico** (Already in public folder)
   - Small icon for browser tabs
   - Should be your business logo

## Image Guidelines:

- **High quality** - Images represent your business
- **Text readable** - Include business name and services
- **Brand consistent** - Use your business colors and fonts
- **Professional** - Clean, modern design

## How to Create:

1. Use Canva, Photoshop, or similar design tool
2. Include your business name "FixIt Appliances"
3. Add text like "Expert Appliance Repair Services"
4. Use your brand colors (check src/styles/main.css for colors)
5. Save as JPG format
6. Place files in this public/images/social/ directory
